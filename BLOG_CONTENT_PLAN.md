## SEO Optimization Checklist (Per Article)

### Before Publishing:
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (first 60 characters)
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2/H3 headings
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars) with keyword
- [ ] URL slug with primary keyword
- [ ] Internal links (3-5 to other posts/pages)
- [ ] External links (2-3 to authoritative sources)
- [ ] Images with descriptive alt text
- [ ] Schema markup (Article schema)
- [ ] Read time estimate
- [ ] Author bio
- [ ] Related posts section
- [ ] Social sharing buttons
- [ ] CTA at end of post

### Content Quality:
- [ ] Minimum 2,000 words
- [ ] Well-structured with headings
- [ ] Bullet points and lists for readability
- [ ] Examples and case studies
- [ ] Actionable advice
- [ ] Original insights
- [ ] Proper grammar and spelling
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting

## Keyword Targeting Strategy

### Primary Keywords (1 per article):
- High search volume (500+)
- Low-medium competition
- High commercial intent
- Relevant to AgentWrite

### Secondary Keywords (3-5 per article):
- Related to primary keyword
- Long-tail variations
- Question-based queries
- LSI keywords

### Long-Tail Keywords (5-10 per article):
- Very specific queries
- Low competition
- High conversion potential
- Natural language

## Publishing Workflow

### Daily Routine:
1. **Morning (9 AM)**: Research and outline article
2. **Midday (12 PM)**: Write first draft
3. **Afternoon (3 PM)**: Edit and optimize for SEO
4. **Evening (6 PM)**: Publish and promote

### Weekly Review:
- Analyze top-performing articles
- Identify content gaps
- Update older articles
- Plan next week's content

## Promotion Strategy

### On Publish:
1. Share on social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook)
2. Post in relevant communities (Reddit, Discord, forums)
3. Email newsletter subscribers
4. Update internal linking from related posts
5. Submit to content aggregators

### Ongoing:
1. Repurpose content (video, infographic, podcast)
2. Update with new information
3. Share in relevant discussions
4. Build backlinks through outreach
5. Monitor and respond to comments
